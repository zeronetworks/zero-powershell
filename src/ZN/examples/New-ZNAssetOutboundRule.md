### Example 1: Create a rule for an asset
```powershell
$asset = Search-ZNAsset -fqdn WC01.posh.local
$sourceasset = Search-ZNAsset -fqdn cs01.posh.local
$portsList = New-ZNPortsList -Protocol ICMP
New-ZNAssetOutboundRule -AssetId $asset -Action 1 -LocalEntityId $asset -LocalProcessesList @("*") -PortsList $portsList -RemoteEntityIdsList @($sourceAsset) -State 1                                                                                                       
```
