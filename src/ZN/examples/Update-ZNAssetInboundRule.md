### Example 1: Update a rule for an asset
```powershell
$asset = Search-ZNAsset -fqdn wc01.posh.local
$rule = Get-ZNAssetInboundRule -Asset $asset | where {$_.Description -eq "Test Rule"}
$rule.RemoteEntityIdsList += (Search-ZNAsset -Fqdn cs01.posh.local)
Update-ZNAssetInboundRule -AssetId $asset -RuleId $rule.id -RemoteEntityIdsList $rule.RemoteEntityIdsList
```
